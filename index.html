About Me

I’m Roba Girma Hurube, a curious mind exploring the universe and how things work, why they work that way, and how we can improve them. My curiosity moves between engineering, astronomy, finance, and philosophy. To me, these fields are connected: they each explore systems—whether they’re physical, human, or conceptual. I enjoy breaking down ideas, understanding patterns, and building solutions that feel intentional and clear. I believe learning is a lifelong process, and I approach every project as a chance to discover something new.
